What this means

Each labeled region is shaded by the average Jul–Sep Atlantic tropical-cyclone exposure of its member states (1851–present, HURDAT2, ≤150 km of the state). Mexico is shown with placeholder seed scores while a Pacific-basin counterpart of HURDAT2 is wired in.

Live NHC forecast cones are drawn on top. A reefer lane is flagged when its path crosses any active cone polygon (turf.js intersect).
